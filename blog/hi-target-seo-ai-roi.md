## Phase 1: High-Target Implementation Audit
Before you scale, you must audit. Most companies fail at **AI ROI and implementation** because they automate the wrong things.
- **Focus**: High-volume, low-complexity tasks.
- **Metric**: Cost-per-task reduction.

## Phase 2: Choosing the Right Stack
Don't buy a Ferrari to go to the grocery store. High-ROI implementations focus on "task-fit" models.
- **LLMs**: Use for creative and high-reasoning tasks.
- **Specialized Agents**: Use for data extraction, monitoring, and repetitive logic.

## Phase 3: Measuring the Bottom Line
To prove **AI ROI and implementation** success, you must track three key KPIs:
1. **Time Saved**: Hours reclaimed for strategic work.
2. **Error Rate Reduction**: Cost savings from precision.
3. **Revenue Acceleration**: Faster turnaround equals more capacity.
